Terms and disclaimer

A guide to first contact, not clinical care.

Care Finder Aotearoa is being prepared for a limited public soft launch. It should be used as a practical navigation aid only.

What this site does

  • Suggests possible first-contact pathways based on your answers.
  • Shows provider and support-service contact information from public sources.
  • Creates an editable first-contact message you can copy or open in your email app.
  • Points to crisis guidance, 1737, 111, WINZ, ACC, and other public support routes.

What this site does not do

  • It does not diagnose a condition.
  • It does not assess clinical risk.
  • It does not replace a doctor, counsellor, psychologist, psychiatrist, crisis team, or emergency service.
  • It does not guarantee that any provider is available, suitable, funded, or taking new clients.
  • It does not send messages on your behalf.

Provider and funding information

Provider details, costs, funding eligibility, waiting times, and referral rules change. Please check directly with the provider, GP practice, WINZ, ACC, Health NZ, or relevant agency before making decisions.

Using links

External websites are controlled by their own organisations. Phone, text, and email links open your own device apps. You are responsible for reviewing any message before sending it.

Corrections and concerns

Please report incorrect, outdated, or unsafe provider information so it can be reviewed.
