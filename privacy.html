Privacy

Your answers are for guidance, not storage.

Care Finder Aotearoa is a navigation aid. It does not provide a medical diagnosis, treatment, emergency triage, or a replacement for professional care.

What this site asks for

The guided contact form asks for enough information to suggest a first contact and prepare a message template. This can include age, gender, address or suburb, what is happening, barriers to care, and support preferences.

What is stored

No account is required. Based on the current site code, your guide answers stay in your browser while you use the page. This static website does not send your mental health answers to a Care Finder server and does not save them to a database.

Address lookup

If you type an address or suburb, the site may use OpenStreetMap Nominatim to find New Zealand address matches and approximate location coordinates. The app also has a local fallback for common towns and suburbs. Address lookup is used to rank nearby providers.

Phone, text, email, and website links

Phone links, text links, and email links open your own phone, SMS, or email app. Email message text is placed into your email app so you can review and edit it before sending. Care Finder does not send the email for you.

Provider information

Provider information can change. Care Finder cannot guarantee that a provider is currently available, taking new clients, eligible for funding, or charging the listed cost. Please confirm details with the provider before relying on them.

Corrections

You can report incorrect phone, website, address, eligibility, cost, or availability information by email.
